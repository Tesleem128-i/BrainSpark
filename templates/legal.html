Your privacy is taken seriously

We collect only what is necessary to operate Brainspark. We do not sell your data, show you ads, or share your information with marketers.

01 — What We Collect

Information We Collect

We collect information you provide when you register and use the Platform:

  • Account info: Name, username, email address, school, country, study level, and profile picture
  • Uploaded files: PDFs you upload to generate quizzes (processed and then deleted from our servers)
  • Usage data: Quiz scores, quiz history, connection activity, and session data
  • Messages: Direct messages and group chat messages sent through the platform
  • Device info: Basic browser and session data for security purposes
02 — How We Use It

How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Create and manage your account
  • Generate personalised quiz questions from your uploaded materials
  • Display your study progress and performance stats on your dashboard
  • Connect you with study buddies based on shared school, country, or level
  • Send transactional emails (verification codes, important notices) via Brevo
  • Detect and prevent abuse, spam, or security threats
AI Processing: Your PDF text is sent to Google Gemini API solely to generate quiz questions. Google's API data handling is governed by their privacy policy. We do not store PDF content beyond the active session.
03 — Third Parties

Third-Party Services We Use

  • AI Engine — Powers quiz generation, tutoring, and explanations
  • Email Provider — Transactional email delivery (verification codes, notices)
  • Cloud Hosting — Secure server and database infrastructure
  • Encrypted Database — Stores your account and quiz data securely

We do not share your data with advertisers, data brokers, or unrelated third parties.

04 — Data Retention

How Long We Keep Your Data

  • Uploaded PDFs: Deleted immediately after quiz generation; text is held in your session only
  • Account data: Retained for as long as your account is active
  • Quiz results: Retained to power your performance dashboard
  • Messages: Retained until you or the recipient deletes the conversation

You may request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time by contacting us.

05 — Security

How We Protect Your Data

  • Passwords are hashed using industry-standard algorithms (never stored in plain text)
  • Database connections use SSL encryption
  • Sessions are signed and stored server-side
  • Email verification is required before account activation

While we implement strong security practices, no system is perfectly invulnerable. We encourage you to use a unique, strong password.

06 — Your Rights

Your Rights Over Your Data

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your account and associated data
  • Object to or restrict certain processing activities
  • Withdraw consent at any time (where processing is consent-based)

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at the email below. We will respond within 30 days.

07 — Cookies

Cookies & Sessions

We use server-side sessions (not third-party tracking cookies) to keep you logged in and remember your preferences (such as light/dark theme). We do not use advertising or analytics cookies.

Privacy concerns or requests?

Contact us and we'll respond within 30 days.

Brainspark is in its early access phase. All features are available to all users at no cost. You will never be charged without being clearly informed and explicitly agreeing to a paid plan.

01 — Current Model

What's Free & What's Included

During the current phase, most Brainspark features are free for all registered users:

  • Study buddy matching and direct messaging
  • Study groups, group chat, polls, and brainstorm sessions
  • Performance dashboard and quiz history
  • Image and document sharing in group chats

The following features require Spark Tokens, which are purchased separately:

  • AI-powered quiz generation from your PDFs
  • AI tutoring and explanations
  • AI group chat assistant
02 — Spark Tokens

How Spark Tokens Work

Spark Tokens power Brainspark's AI features. They are purchased by making a bank transfer and uploading your receipt. Once verified by our team, tokens are credited to your account and can be used immediately.

  • Tokens are purchased via bank transfer — no credit card required
  • Each AI request (quiz generation, tutor chat) deducts tokens from your balance
  • Tokens do not expire
  • The amount you transfer converts directly to tokens after a small processing fee that covers transaction costs
Verification: After you transfer and upload your receipt, our team verifies it manually within a few minutes during business hours and credits your tokens immediately upon confirmation.
03 — Future Plans

What's Coming

We are committed to keeping Brainspark's core social and study features free forever. Our promise to you:

  • Social features — messaging, groups, study buddies — will always be free
  • Token pricing will be clearly displayed before any purchase
  • You will never be automatically charged
  • We will notify you by email before any pricing policy changes
Our promise: You will never be surprised by a charge. Every token purchase is voluntary and initiated by you.
03 — No Ads

Ad-Free Experience

04 — Refund Policy

  • Monthly subscriptions may be cancelled at any time; service continues until the end of the billing period

  • We will publish a detailed refund policy alongside any paid plan announcement
05 — Cancellation

Cancelling Your Account

You may stop using Brainspark at any time. Since there are no paid plans yet, there is nothing to cancel beyond deleting your account. When paid plans exist, you will be able to cancel from your account settings with no cancellation fees.
